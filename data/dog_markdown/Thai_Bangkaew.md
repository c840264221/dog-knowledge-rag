## 🧾 历史
The Thai Bangkaew Dog is an old breed that originated from the Bangkaew village, Thanang-ngam area, Bang-rakam district, and Phitsanulok province in Thailand. The breed traces its ancestry back to a cross between a Buddhist abbot's local black & white female dog and a now-extinct wild dog producing today's Bangkaew breed. In 1957, selective breeding from single litters produced the generations seen today. The Thai Bangkaew Dog is regarded as a precious heritage of Phitsanulok province. The dogs are bred widely in the province and have become so famous nationwide that they now are bred in every part of Thailand.

## 🩺 养护指南
### Health
General health in the breed is still being assessed with health testing not being standard procedure in the country of origin. On the whole they appear a very healthy, long-lived breed not prone to cancer or other debilitating diseases but with the biggest concern currently being early onset eye conditions - PRA, etc. Few instances of hip dysplasia have been noted but as a medium sized breed, it is still recommended as a standard test moving forward(Any problems/diseases, etc)

### Grooming
Weekly brushing with monthly baths or otherwise as needed, and bi-weekly or monthly nail trims. They are not prone to ear infections but ears may still be wiped during regular grooming. Recommended brushes include rake combs and slicker brushes. Do not use a furminator style brush as it can damage the top coat.

### Exercise
Moderate exercise is required as a medium energy breed. They enjoy regular walks, hiking, and supervised off-leash time in an enclosed area. Due to the breed¿s prey drive and independent nature, it is recommended they remain on-leash or in a fenced area.

### Training
Bangkaew typically enjoy training with their owners but do best with a gentle hand and positive reinforcement. Training should be enthusiastic and engaging without much repetition or they may lose interest. Training and socialization should start at a young age. The breed shows potential as a sport dog in venues like fastcat, scentwork, and potentially even dock diving as they have a natural affinity for water. Mental stimulation in the form of puzzle toys and other long-lasting chews are a great addition to their daily routine.
